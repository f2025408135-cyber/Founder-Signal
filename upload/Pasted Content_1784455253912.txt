<section class="page">
  <div class="page-header">
    <div class="kicker">page 06 <span class="dot">·</span> the cold-start solution</div>
    <h2 class="page-title">A founder with zero track record gets a fair, wide-confidence score — not a penalized low one.</h2>
    <p class="page-sub">This is the single highest-scoring differentiator in the brief. The default failure mode is to score absence as zero. We score uncertainty as uncertainty — and surface it visibly to the investor.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="coldstart-grid">
    <div class="coldstart-left">
      <p class="lead">We score <span class="em">uncertainty</span>, not <span class="em">absence</span>.</p>

      <div class="principles">
        <div class="principle">
          <span class="pnum">01</span>
          <div class="pbody">
            <div class="ptitle">Confidence intervals, not point estimates.</div>
            <div class="ptext">Every axis returns a score and a confidence band. A data-rich founder gets a tight band; a cold-start founder gets a wide band centered on the neutral prior. The wide band is the truth — not a low score.</div>
          </div>
        </div>
        <div class="principle">
          <span class="pnum">02</span>
          <div class="pbody">
            <div class="ptitle">Missing feature ≠ feature zero.</div>
            <div class="ptext">Absence of GitHub activity is not evidence of no technical skill. The model imputes a neutral prior with wide variance, then narrows it as evidence accrues. We never collapse missing data into a low number.</div>
          </div>
        </div>
        <div class="principle">
          <span class="pnum">03</span>
          <div class="pbody">
            <div class="ptitle">Evidence accrues — intervals shrink.</div>
            <div class="ptext">A first GitHub commit, a first HN launch, a first accelerator cohort — each new signal narrows the band. The investor sees the founder's score evolve over time, not just the latest snapshot.</div>
